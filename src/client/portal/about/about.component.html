About us

WTH!APPS is a technology start-up.
We build simple, yet powerful, products to help people connect and organize their digital lives.

"WTH" is our story?

Our inspiration came from one short familiar phrase, "WTH! – What the Hell!"

This can be a cry of frustration: "WTH! Why is this not working, so complicated and painful!? Why are there so many pop-ups and ads!? Why can’t I have online privacy?!"

Or it can be a cry for change; a call to take up the challenge to make things better: "WTH! Let’s fix this! Let’s make it better!"

We took our collective frustrations and annoyances with the Internet and turned it into something positive: WTH!Apps!

WTH!APPS is a stress-free and enjoyable place for the complete management of your digital life.

We built it with these aims in mind:

  • Simplify your digital life
  • Keep your social media, chat and everything else in your digital life in one place
  • Create Easy-to-use Apps
  • Remove unwanted distractions
  • Consolidate, organize and easily search all your digital stuff
  • Create a peaceful and fun online environment

Our mission in a nutshell

  • Give you all the online tools you need to have a simpler, peaceful, productive and fun digital life
  • Enable you to easily and securely connect with your family, friends, community and coworkers
  • Help you organize all your digital stuff
  • Provide a private and ad free online WTH!Zone
  • Make the internet a fun place again

Our values in a nutshell

  • Simplicity
  • Functionality
  • Peacefulness
  • Privacy
  • Friends, Family and Community
  • Fun
